This visualisation shows some calendar information from the employees of an imaginary company. The rectangles are meetings and the ovals are people. A connecting line means that the person has attended a meeting.

You can zoom in and out and move nodes around.

Try searching for 'cloud' or 'neural': all meetings where the agenda or the title contains the search term will be highlighted, as well as the people who attended. Mouseover a node to see information about it. The idea is to show the spread of ideas in a company and to find the relevant people for an idea or an issue. See the code here
